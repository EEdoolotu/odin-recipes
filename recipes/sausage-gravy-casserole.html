Sausage Gravy Casserole

Description

Sausage Gravy Casserole is a hearty breakfast bake featuring flaky biscuits layered with savory sausage and creamy, peppered gravy, all baked until warm and comforting. It’s rich, satisfying, and perfect for slow mornings or feeding a hungry crowd.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package premium pork sausage (such as Jimmy Dean)
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon chicken bouillon powder, or more to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons quick-mixing all-purpose flour, or more to taste
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon unsalted butter, or as needed
  • 1 (10 count) package refrigerated buttermilk biscuits

Steps

  1. Cook sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring with a spatula and breaking sausage apart as it cooks, 5 to 7 minutes. Stir in onion powder, garlic powder, bouillon granules, and black pepper until well combined and sausage fully cooked, 5 to 7 minutes more.
  2. Stir in flour until mixture coated, then stir in milk and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer; stir until thickened, 5 to 7 minutes. Taste; adjust seasonings. Set aside.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter; set aside.
  4. Cut each biscuit into four pieces. Arrange ½ biscuits in bottom of the prepared baking dish; cover with sausage-gravy mixture. Top with remaining ½ biscuits.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are fully cooked and golden brown, 13 to 15 minutes.
  6. Set aside before serving for 3 to 5 minutes.
