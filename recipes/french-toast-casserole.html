French Toast Casserole

Description

French Toast Casserole is a sweet, oven-baked breakfast made with cubes of bread soaked in a cinnamon-spiced custard, then baked until golden and fluffy. It’s easy to prepare ahead of time and perfect for cozy mornings, brunches, or holiday gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups French bread cubes
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¼ cup white sugar, divided
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
  2. Line the bottom of the prepared pan with bread cubes.
  3. Beat milk, eggs, 2 tablespoons sugar, salt, and vanilla together in a large bowl until well combined; pour over bread cubes and stir to coat. Dot with butter; let stand for 15 minutes.
  4. Combine remaining 2 tablespoons sugar with cinnamon; sprinkle over top of casserole.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the center is set and top is golden, about 45 to 50 minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
